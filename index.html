About Me

I am a doctoral student at the University of Bern since 2022, working on AI in nutrition as part of the ARTORG Center’s AI in Health and Nutrition Lab .

I hold an MSc in Data Science with Distinction (2022) from the Asia Pacific University of Technology and Innovation (APU), and a BSc in Information Technology with First Class Honours from APU in partnership with Staffordshire University.

Previously, I worked at SWIFT as a Systems and Application Management and Support Specialist, where I gained extensive experience in SAP Basis, SAP GRC, Linux administration, and enterprise IT operations.

Research

My PhD research integrates computer vision and recent AI advances for automatic dietary assessment and personalized context-aware nutrition. Key contributions include works on semi-automatic food segmentation, tackling the Out of distribution problem in food recognition, depth-based food volume estimation, and the development of Food Knowledge Graphs for personalized nutrition recommendations.
