About Me

I am a doctoral student at the University of Bern since 2022, working on applying AI in nutrition as part of ARTORG Center’s AI in Health and Nutrition Lab . I hold an MSc in Data Science with Distinction (2022) and a BSc in Information Technology with First Class Honours from Asia Pacific University of Technology and Innovation (APU).

I also have industry experience from 2019 to 2022, during which I worked at SWIFT as a Systems and Application Management and Support Specialist, where I gained experience in SAP Basis, SAP GRC, and basic Linux administration.

Currently as a PhD student, my research combines computer vision and recent AI advances for automatic dietary assessment and personalized context-aware nutrition. Key contributions include works on semi-automatic food segmentation , tackling the Out of distribution problem in food recognition , depth-based food volume estimation , and the development of Food Knowledge Graphs for personalized nutrition recommendations .
