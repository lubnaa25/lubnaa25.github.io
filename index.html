About Me

I am a doctoral student at the University of Bern since 2022, working on applying AI in nutrition. I hold an MSc in Data Science with Distinction (2022) and a BSc in Information Technology with First Class Honours from Asia Pacific University, Malaysia.

Previously, I worked at SWIFT as a Systems and Application Management and Support Specialist, where I gained extensive experience in SAP Basis, SAP GRC, Linux administration, and enterprise IT operations.

Research

My PhD research advances AI-driven dietary assessment by integrating computer vision, and recent AI advances for personalized context-aware nutrition. Key contributions include works on semi-automatic food segmentation, tackling the Out of distribution problem in food recognition, and depth-based food volume estimation and the development of Food Knowledge Graphs for personalized nutrition recommendations.
